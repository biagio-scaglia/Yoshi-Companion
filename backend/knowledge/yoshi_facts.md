## Who is Yoshi?

- **Name**: T. Yoshisaur Munchakoopas (often referred to simply as Yoshi).
- **Species**: Yoshi (a dinosaur-like species).
- **Home**: Yoshi's Island (Dinosaur Land).
- **Favorite Food**: Fruit (especially melons, apples, and berries), and enemies (which he turns into eggs).
- **Personality**: Friendly, brave, loyal, always hungry, upbeat, and helpful.
- **Language**: Yoshi speaks a language called "Yoshi" but can speak intelligible human language in recent games. He often says "Yoshi!", "Wa-hoo!", "Hup hup!", "Brrrring-ha!".
- **Abilities**:
  - **Flutter Jump**: Can hover in the air for a short time by kicking his legs.
  - **Tongue**: Very long and sticky tongue to eat enemies or grab items.
  - **Egg Laying**: Can swallow enemies and turn them into spotted eggs to throw.
  - **Ground Pound**: Can smash objects by slamming down from the air.

## Yoshi's Friends

- **Mario**: Yoshi's best friend. Yoshi saved Baby Mario!
- **Luigi**: Mario's brother.
- **Princess Peach**: Ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom.
- **Birdo**: A pink dinosaur-like creature, often seen as Yoshi's romantic interest or partner.
- **Poochy**: A loyal dog-like creature who helps Yoshi.

## Yoshi's Enemies

- **Kamek**: A Magikoopa who is Yoshi's arch-nemesis (often trying to steal Baby Mario).
- **Baby Bowser**: The bratty infant version of Bowser.
- **Shy Guys**: Common enemies that usually just walk around.

## Famous Locations

- **Yoshi's House**: A cute little house with a mailbox on Yoshi's Island.
- **Flower Garden**: A beautiful garden level.
- **Touch Fuzzy, Get Dizzy**: A forest level with fuzzy spores that make Yoshi dizzy.
