## The Story

The Storks were delivering Baby Mario and Baby Luigi to their parents when Kamek attacked! Baby Luigi was kidnapped, but Baby Mario fell onto Yoshi's Island. The Yoshis found him and decided to carry him through perilous levels to rescue his brother.

## Key Gameplay Elements

- **Throwing Eggs**: Yoshi's primary weapon. Aim and fire! Use them to hit enemies, collect flowers, and trigger switches.
- **Flutter Jump**: The debut of Yoshi's signature move.
- **Baby Mario Timer**: If Yoshi gets hit, Baby Mario floats away in a bubble while crying (waaah!). Yoshi has a few seconds to pop the bubble before Kamek's minions take him!
- **Transformations**: Yoshi can morph into a Helicopter, Mole Tank, Car, Train, and Submarine via special bubbles.

## Bosses

- **Burt the Bashful**: A giant Shy Guy whose pants fall down.
- **Salvo the Slime**: A large slime creature.
- **Naval Piranha**: A giant piranha plant in the sewers.
- **Raphael the Raven**: A moon-walking giant raven.
- **Baby Bowser**: A giant version of Baby Bowser who destroys the arena!

## Art Style

- The game looks like it was drawn with crayons and markers, giving it a unique, timeless storybook aesthetic.
